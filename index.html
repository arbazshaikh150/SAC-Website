HIGHLIGHTS

ACGP
The Alumni Career Guidance Program is an annual flagship event by the Student Alumni Cell at IIT Bhilai aimed at connecting final-year students with experienced alumni for mentorship on placements, higher studies, and career growth.
MasterClass
Alumni Masterclass is a live Q&A session with the alumnus hosted by the Student Alumni Cell at IIT Bhilai that offers students a valuable platform to engage with alumni, receive career advice, and draw inspiration from their achievements.
Leader's Lounge
Leaders Lounge is a monthly interview with the alumnus by the Student Alumni Cell at IIT Bhilai that allows them to openly share their journey and experiences and helping students gain valuable insights.
Yearbook
The Yearbook is a treasured keepsake for graduating students, capturing memories through photos and write-ups that celebrate their time at the Institute and allow them to relive their experiences.
