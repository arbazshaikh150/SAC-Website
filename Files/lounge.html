Leader’s Lounge

Leader’s Lounge is one of the unique flagship events of Student Alumni Cell, IIT Bhilai in which one of the alumni is interviewed by the members of the Student Alumni Cell. Owing to its nature, Alumni feels free to share their experiences about their journey so far. The alumni interview is aimed at leveraging the benefits of the alumni’s personal experiences, which could act as a guiding source of inspiration for the current student community.
