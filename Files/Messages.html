Messages to the Alumni

  • Director
  • Dean of Alumni Affairs
Prof. Rajeev Prakah
Director, IIT Bhilai
Dear IIT Bhilaians,

It gives me immense pleasure writing to you. Let me first of all congratulate you on this remarkable achievement of yours as we hold the convocation for the graduating class of 2023. As you graduate from IIT Bhilai, you will now be our ambassadors as alumni and more especially a torchbearer being one of the early batches of the institute.

My best wishes to all of you to keep moving forward and excel in all your future endeavors. This is the time that you will be part of the socio-economic fabric of the country. Your knowledge acquired at IIT Bhilai should be focused on giving back to society and the nation as a whole. I am sure that you will take a leading role in time to come and be fully devoted with all your efforts towards the pious task of nation-building.
Dr. Dhiman Saha
Dean of Alumni Affairs, IIT Bhilai
Dear class of 2023,

On behalf of the IIT Bhilai fraternity, I congratulate each one of you on your outstanding achievement of graduating from this institute.

You have achieved this success through your hard work, dedication, and perseverance. We are sure that the knowledge, wisdom, and virtues that you have gathered at IIT Bhilai would help you bring laurels not only to yourself but also to your family, institution, and the nation.

You belong to a special batch who had to deal with the COVID-19 pandemic. But you have shown incredible strength, resilience, and determination in this hour of crisis and have proven that everything is possible with hard work, commitment, and patience.

In the words of Confucius, "Wherever you go, go with all your heart." We all believe that you would be an asset not only for your alma mater but for the country at large.
